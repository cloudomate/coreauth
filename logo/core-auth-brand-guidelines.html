04 — Spacing
Clear Space
Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "c" in the wordmark. This ensures the mark is never crowded by surrounding elements.
1x 1x 1x 1x
c re . auth

The clear space unit (1x) equals the cap height of "c" in the logo. Scale proportionally with the logo.

06 — Tone of Voice
How We Sound
core.auth speaks with quiet confidence. We don't oversell — we let precision and reliability do the talking. Our voice reflects the same values as our visual identity: clarity, trust, and technical depth.
Precise Confident Minimal Human

Precise, not verbose

Say exactly what's needed, nothing more. Every word should earn its place. Technical accuracy matters — never approximate when you can be exact.

Confident, not arrogant

We know what we've built is solid. State facts directly without hedging, but never talk down to users or competitors. Let the product speak.

Minimal, not cold

Brevity shows respect for people's time. But minimal doesn't mean robotic — warmth lives in clarity. Help people feel secure, not processed.

Human, not casual

We protect real people's identities. Speak like a trusted engineer, not a chatbot. Professional but approachable, technical but never alienating.

We say

"Authenticate in under 200ms with zero-knowledge proof."

"Your identity. Your device. Nothing stored."

"Built for developers who ship."

We don't say

"The most revolutionary, game-changing auth solution ever!"

"We're disrupting the identity space!"

"Super easy, anyone can do it!"
